Note: this is the client app for the self-hostable Immich Server. You will need to run/manage the server on your own in order to use the app.

Easily back up, organize, and manage your photos on your own server. Immich helps you browse, search and organize your photos and videos with ease, without sacrificing your privacy.

<b>Features:</b>

* Upload and view photos and videos
* Automatically backup assets on your device
* Supports almost all image formats, including raw and live/motion photos
* Extract and display exif metadata (lens, make, model, tags, location, etc.)
* Search by metadata, objects, faces, and CLIP
* Extensive sharing functionality
* Find people using facial recognition
* And much more
