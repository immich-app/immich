Discovery Panel — Independent of Nav

The nav sidebar stays untouched. A new collapsible + resizable panel houses all discovery filters.

Left Discovery Panel — Left Side
A new 240px panel sits between the nav sidebar and the photo grid. Collapsible to a 32px icon strip. Resizable via drag handle. Only rendered on the /discover route — zero changes to existing nav.
Zero modifications to existing nav sidebar Natural left-to-right flow: nav → filters → results Collapse to icons when you want more photo space Resize handle lets users find their preferred width
~400px total left-side width (nav + panel) when expanded Two panels side by side on left can feel heavy
